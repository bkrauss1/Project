Project Proposal

What is your website about?

My website is about running and injury prevention.

What is the objective of your website? (Also read as why would this site be important?)

The objctive of this website is to create awareness and provide information for injury prevention targeted towards runners. This is important for all types of runners, and I think it would be helpful to have post-run information.

Who is your target audience set?

The target audience for this website is runners of all levels. From beginners to college athletes, I want to provide helpful information that applies to all runners of different skill levels.

What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?

I plan to have a stretching page, injury- recovery page, helpful running tips and tricks, and healthy habits page as the main content of this website.
